Subjects Offered: From LKG to 12th Grade

Detailed curriculum overview following Samacheer Kalvi

  • LKG: Languages (Tamil/English basics), Numbers 1-20, Colors and Shapes, Basic Rhymes and Stories
  • UKG: Enhanced language skills, Simple arithmetic, Environmental awareness, Social skills
  • Class 1: Tamil & English basics, Numbers up to 100, Environmental Science
  • Class 2: Basic Math, Language development, Surroundings awareness
  • Class 3: Grammar intro, Multiplication tables, Plants and Animals
  • Class 4: Fractions, Comprehension, General Science
  • Class 5: Basic Geometry, Measurement, Science experiments
  • Class 6: Algebra basics, History & Geography, Practical Science
  • Class 7: Arithmetic, Literature, Physics intro, Technology
  • Class 8: Geometry, Environmental Science, Social Science concepts
  • Class 9: Algebra, Geometry, Physics, Chemistry, Biology basics, Civics & History
  • Class 10: Coordinate Geometry, Reactions, Advanced Biology, Social analysis

Choose from Commerce, Computer Science, or Biology streams.

Commerce Stream

  • Core: Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, Commerce Principles, English, Tamil
  • Optional: Mathematics / Computer Applications
  • Career: CA, MBA, Commerce, Banking

Computer Science Stream

  • Core: Computer Science, Maths, Physics, Chemistry, English, Tamil
  • Career: Engineering, Software, IT

Biology Stream

  • Core: Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology, English, Tamil
  • Optional: Mathematics
  • Career: Medicine, Biotech, Research
